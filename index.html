  • Apartment-level ownership and tenancy records
  • Association member management & roles
  • Community announcements and updates
  • Polls and resident feedback collection
  • Event management and booking
  • Amenity scheduling and access control

Finance & Billing

  • Automated maintenance due generation
  • Monthly collection tracking by unit
  • Pending dues with aging report
  • Expense recording and categorization
  • Vendor payment tracking
  • Board-level financial summary reports
  • Collection rate monitoring by tower

Operations & Maintenance

  • Daily task planning with assignment
  • DG run-hour and diesel consumption logs
  • Water level monitoring (raw & treated)
  • Gas cylinder inventory (full / empty)
  • Work order creation and tracking
  • Manpower shift and duty management
  • Repeat task scheduling

  • Visitor entry logging by gate
  • Pre-authorized visitor pass generation
  • Security incident reporting and tracking
  • CCTV camera oversight and logs
  • Parcel receipt and delivery tracking
  • Resident notification on parcel arrival
  • Shift handover reports for security staff

Communication & Cases

  • Push announcements to all residents

  • Case management with status tracking

Staff & Management

Unified Workflow

How a community manager starts their day

One login. Complete situational awareness. Everything in the right sequence.

1

Open Dashboard

See total units, residents, collection status, and pending dues at a glance. No digging.

2

Check Utilities

Review DG run hours, water tank levels, and gas cylinder counts. Flag anything below threshold.

3

Review Today's Plan

9 tasks already assigned. Pipe work on Well-4, Tower 3 leakage, fire alarm checks. Assign teams.

4

Handle Complaints

Open the Cases module. Flat 4032 seepage is pending follow-up. Update status and notify resident.

5

Track Collections

₹4.25L in pending dues. Filter by tower. Send reminders to overdue units directly from the platform.

6

Close & Report

Mark completed tasks. Log expenses incurred. Generate end-of-day summary for the board.

Four pillars that make communities choose Zoho Nest

🔍

Transparency

Every financial transaction, maintenance log, and decision is recorded and accessible to the right stakeholders. Your residents and board see exactly what's happening.

✅

Accountability

Tasks are assigned to named individuals. Complaints route to responsible parties. Every action is timestamped. Nothing falls through the cracks because someone thought someone else was handling it.

🔒

Data Security

Resident data, financial records, and legal documents are stored with role-based access controls. Staff see only what their role requires. Your sensitive data stays that way.

🚀

Adoption

Designed for communities where staff may not be tech-savvy. The interface is direct, in plain language, with minimal clicks to complete common tasks. If your team can use a phone, they can use Zoho Nest.

Maintenance dues are configured per unit or per flat type. The system automatically generates monthly bills, tracks payments, and flags overdue accounts.
